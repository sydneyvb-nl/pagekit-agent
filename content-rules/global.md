# Global content rules

These rules apply to all generated copy, in every vertical. They override any
stylistic preference.

## Always

- Separate **known facts** (from the brief) from **assumptions**. Record every
  assumption in `generated/assumptions.md`.
- Add an explicit `TODO:` marker for any missing fact, and list it in
  `generated/missing-inputs.md`.
- Write in the brief's `language`. Match the `brand.voice`.
- For every service page, answer: **what it is · who it's for · how it works ·
  what to expect · when to contact · the next step.**
- For every local page, include: city · service area · a contact route · at
  least two internal links.

## Never invent

- credentials, certifications, awards
- testimonials, review counts, review scores
- opening hours
- pricing
- team members
- medical or legal guarantees
- regulatory / compliance claims

When any of the above is needed but absent, use a clearly-marked placeholder and
a `TODO`. A plausible-sounding invention is a defect, not a convenience.

## Tone

- Concrete over generic. "Echo's vanaf 7 weken" beats "professionele zorg".
- No hype, no superlatives you can't substantiate ("de beste", "#1").
- Short sentences. Active voice. Accessible reading level.

See also: `regulated-industries.md`, `tone-of-voice.md`, and the selected
vertical's `content_cautions`.
