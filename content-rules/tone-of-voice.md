# Tone of voice

Derive tone from `business.brand.voice`, constrained by these defaults.

| Voice cue        | Do                                              | Don't                                  |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| warm             | personal, reassuring, plain words               | saccharine, over-familiar              |
| professional     | clear, confident, specific                      | jargon, corporate filler               |
| accessible       | short sentences, common words, define terms     | clever wordplay that obscures meaning  |

## Universal defaults

- Address the reader directly ("u" / "je" per language and audience; keep it
  consistent across the site).
- One idea per sentence. Prefer verbs over nominalizations.
- Lead with the reader's need, then the business's answer.
- Headings describe value, not features ("Snel terecht voor een echo", not
  "Echodienst").
- No exclamation-mark spam, no ALL CAPS, no emoji in body copy.
